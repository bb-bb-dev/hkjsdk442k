TyreWiz pressure and alerts on Garmin

Know your pressure before it costs you the ride

TubeWitch for TyreWiz is a Garmin data field for Quarq TyreWiz 1.0 and 2.0 sensors, Quarq TyreWiz for Moto sensors, and Zipp 303 SW wheels with the integrated Zipp AXS Wheel Sensor. It puts live tire pressure on Garmin Edge devices and Garmin watches, with clear warnings, fast reconnect behavior, and support for multiple bikes or wheelsets.

  • See front and rear pressure together at a glance
  • React earlier with popup, audible, and vibration alerts
  • Keep up to five named bike or wheelset profiles ready

Ride confidence

Know when pressure really changed

Ever think a tire felt low after a hard hit, then stop to check but everything was fine? TubeWitch shows whether that brutal rock strike, thorns, or debris actually caused pressure loss, so you can avoid unnecessary stops and keep riding with confidence. If you really are losing air, TubeWitch will give you audible and popup alerts based on real pressure data, so you can bail before hitting that next big jump or descent.

TubeWitch can even keep watch in the background, so you can leave your map or favorite screen up and still get audible and popup alerts.

Fastest path

Setup once, then ride

TubeWitch is designed for a short pre-ride setup session: enter IDs, add the field, start an activity, and confirm both wheels report before rollout.

  1. Install using the Garmin Connect IQ mobile app and reboot Garmin.
  2. Enter front and rear sensor IDs in TubeWitch settings. View Demo
  3. Add TubeWitch to a data page and start an activity.
  4. Rotate wheels and confirm live pressure appears.
